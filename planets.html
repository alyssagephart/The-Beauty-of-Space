PLANETS

There are an unknown number of planets out there in outer space, but only 8 in our specific solar system. Technically, Pluto is now counted as a Dwarf Planet, along with Eris, Haumea, Makemake, and Ceres in the Solar System. There are also 4 Terrestrial (Rocky) Planets, 2 Gas Giants, and 2 Ice Giants.

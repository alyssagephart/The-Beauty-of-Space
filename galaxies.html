GALAXIES

Did you know that there are so many other galaxies than the one we live in? The Milky Way is pretty iconic, but there are so many others our telescoping technology has viewed before.

They can be categorized based on their shapes and physical features, by their central activity (supermassive black hole!), or by the angle we must look at to observe them.
